Case Study: Philippines

Guide and Build Patient Awareness for You! (GABAY) - Patient Navigation Program

Background

  • There is delayed institutionalization of Health Technology Assessment (HTA) leading to deferred decisions for public reimbursement post national formulary inclusion.
  • However, in lieu of this there is increased budget allocation by the government which can be disbursed through special funds.

Project Goal

Based on a robust assessment of HCS, we understood the opportunity to mitigate the barrier of information asymmetry for patients. To support patients understand the process and documentary requirements to get access to the available funding sources from the public system.

Key Learnings

Understanding and assessing the external landscape is a pre-requisite to identify the right opportunity and to act on it.

Selecting the right partner is key.

Seamless Collaboration holds the key.

Developing a clear execution plan and KPIs are critical.
