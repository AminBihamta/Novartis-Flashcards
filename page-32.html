Guide and Build Patient Awareness for You! (GABAY) - Patient Navigation Program

Project Details/Solution

Introducing Novartis Connect - Launched in Q1 2025

Novartis Connect is a new and exciting initiative to improve patient and prescriber access to private medicines. It includes two main components:

Step 1

Patients to reach out through GABAY contact details. Once consent is provided, GABAY to call patients and provide guidance on social funding.

Step 2

Patients to accomplish requirements and approaches relevant government agency . GABAY can provide follow up calls as support.

Step 3

GABAY to inform patients where to send Guarantee Letters and documents for medicine fulfillment. Following consent provided, GABAY can call patient regarding availability of next funding cycle.

Impact so far:

Supported 1500 new patients across brands

Supported 2700+ funding approvals as grant letters

50% of current patients have at least 3 funding grant letters

Sales value since launch (April 2024) is $4.5Mn.

Faster avg. approval time from >30 days to <2weeks.
