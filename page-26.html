Novartis Connect: A centralised self-pay program broadening patient and healthcare institution access across the full pharmaceutical portfolio

Project Details/Solution

Introducing Novartis Connect - Launched in Q1 2025

Novartis Connect is a new and exciting initiative to improve patient and prescriber access to private medicines. It includes two main components:

Novartis Private Prescription Program (NPPP)

This program connects patients who have been prescribed a Novartis medicine with an e-pharmacy platform, supported by Terry White Chemmart, to dispense the product. It aims to broaden access, choice, and convenience for those who rely on private prescription medicines. To ensure price confidentiality (e.g., patients sharing prices on social media), all discounted products are wrapped up in a deal structure such as "Buy X, Get Y." The program will also allow patients to utilize 'Pay in 4' or split payment alternatives to assist in their purchasing (roll-out in Q4 2025).

Hospital Ordering Program (HOP)

This program provides healthcare institutions with an online ordering and delivery experience for inpatient and outpatient private script needs. It is supported by an eCommerce platform, operated by Akesa, and is primarily used by Directors of Pharmacy in hospital settings. The HOP provides off-invoice discounting on nominated products to assist healthcare providers in making informed decisions about their patients' treatment options and level of affordability.

Results to date:

Successfully launched Kisqali eBC and guaranteed patient access from Day Zero.

Expanded program to 30+ product SKUs.

Successfully enrolled 15 patients in the first 6 months of launch.
