Driving Sybrava (Leqvio) Growth Through Strategic Utilization of Private Health Insurance

Project Details/Solution

Given the challenges and extended negotiation periods for government reimbursement for Leqvio, we have strategically pivoted to leveraging private health insurance as an alternative funding source by employing a targeted 2-part solution.

Part 1: Strategic Utilisation of Private Health Insurance

  • Leveraging local private health insurance effectively removes the financial barriers to treatment initiation. By covering the first few doses of Leqvio, patients can start their treatment journey with a peace of mind.
  • Our reps are trained to educate doctors on private health insurance post-hospitalization coverage for treatments such as Leqvio. This empowers doctors to assure their patients about insurance support, helping them overcome cost-related hurdles.
  • By strategically leveraging private health insurance and providing targeted education to doctors, up to the first 3 doses of Leqvio can be covered.

Part 2: Complementary Patient Support Program

To complement insurance coverage, we have also introduced a Patient Support Program aimed at increasing long-term adherence and reducing drop-out rates when insurance coverage ceases. This ensures continuity of treatment and optimized patient outcomes.

The strategic utilization of private health insurance has been instrumental in driving Leqvio market growth while the provision of supplementary support ensures continued care and long-term adherence, sustaining patient outcomes.
