Case Study: Singapore

Driving Sybrava (Leqvio) Growth Through Strategic Utilization of Private Health Insurance

Background

  • Our journey to secure government reimbursement for Leqvio has been met with multiple challenges and extended negotiation periods.
  • These obstacles highlighted the need for a robust alternative growth strategy in the self-pay market across both private and public channels.
  • Recognizing the potential for significant growth in this self-pay segment, we deep dive into approaches that will drive the use of Leqvio. One of the approaches involve the strategic utilization of private health insurance to cover for treatment with Leqvio.

Project Goal

  • Capitalizing on this significant source, we aim to enhance access to Leqvio and drive Leqvio growth and utilization in the self-pay market across both private and public channels.

Key Learnings

A deep understanding of the various funding sources and how they apply to different patient segments is critical.

Mapping the patient journey is vital to identify key levers and drivers that will facilitate Leqvio adoption and improve adherence.

Many physicians have limited awareness of insurance benefits and coverage. It is critical to educate them, as well-informed doctors can engage in more effective conversations with their patients to leverage the utilization of their insurance policies.
