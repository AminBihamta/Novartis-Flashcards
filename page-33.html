Case Study: Philippines

SYmple Pay - Fintech Partnership

Background

  • Cash-Flow Challenge for Patient.
    HCPs / Patients are hesitant for spending on high price medicines.
  • Is The Price Worth It? WTP.
    High discount / lower price is the only way to sell in a self-pay / out of pocket market.
  • Challenging The Status-Quo.
    Significant investment is needed Patient Programs (PAP) to drive adherence & compliance.

Project Goal

  • Enables high cost of per injection to be spread over several months payment aka Monthly installment plan.
  • Value-for-money discount for upfront commitment of 3 injections – intent is to increase treatment compliance

Key Learnings

Discount to Patient ≠ PAP/PSP -> work with ERC, Legal and Finance to design the operational process based on your goal

Co-develop the solution with your CFT

Onboarding partners (Retailers & FinTech) on the “Vision / Purpose” is very important

Pilot a solution early (soft-launch) - fail-fast and adapt-fast.
