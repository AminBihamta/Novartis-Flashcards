Project Details/Solution

SYbscription Voucher Model:

  • Encourage Patients to decide & commit on SYBRAVA - price differentiation (38% discount when buying 3 injections upfront).
  • Ability to monitor Patient journey without extensive investment on Patient programs while respecting personal data privacy regulations.
  • Improve Compliance of Patients as we addressed affordability, cash flow and making it convenient.

SYmple Operations:

  • eVouchers - Online purchase or at Retailers location.
  • Intelligent activation 1st coupon needs to used before 2nd is activated - allows us to monitor the treatment stages.
  • Time bound - patients must adhere to doses as each coupon expires based on treatment schedule.
