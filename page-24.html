Leveraging Private Health Insurance to improve access for Sybrava (Leqvio)

Project Details/Solution

Healthcare Provider Engagement

The program launched with targeted education and training on new insurance guidelines and Sybrava's benefits, boosting provider confidence in prescribing by highlighting improved claim approval rates.

Insurance Executive Training

Targeted sessions equipped insurance executives with in-depth understanding of Sybrava's claims process, resulting in more accurate and efficient claim adjudication.

Third Party Administrators (TPAs)

In-depth orientation on the disease and Sybrava therapy enabled TPAs to make informed claim decisions, significantly reducing rejection rates.

Hospital Administrators

Focused awareness initiatives equipped administrators with clarity on new insurance options, encouraging confident adoption of Sybrava therapy backed by assured claims support.

The program's success was evident month by month,

Rejection rate plummeting to 30% by December 2024, from 70% in September 2024.

PHI as a channel contributed to ~21% (1086) patients of the overall Sybrava self-pay patient pool.
